1. Instruction of controlling mobile phone from PC:
    1.1 Instruction of turning on USB debugging:
        if your device is Redmi Note 8
        Go to settings => About Phone
        and push MUI Version 7 times untill
        it is shown that "No need, you are 
        already a developer"
        if your device is not Redmi Note 8
        look for from the internet how to 
        turn on a developer mode for your device model

    1.2 Downloading Desktop app of mobile controller 
        Windows orqali mobil telefonni PC ga 
        kabelli ulangan holatda PC dan turib 
        nazorat qilish uchun kerak bo'lgan
        Windows ilovasi uchun link:
            https://github.com/Genymobile/scrcpy/tree/master

        CTRL + F => "Get the app"
        qidiriladi va sahifadan Windows linkini 
        ustiga bosiladi.

        "From the official release" qismidan
        64 bitli zip yuklab olinadi.

        ZiP fayl ichiga kiring, va Mobil qurilmangizni
        PC ga USB kabel orqali ulagan holatda 
        zip file ichidan scripy.exe ni ustiga 2 marta
        bosing.

        Va sizning ilovangiz ekranda namoyon boladi.

2. Instruction of Creating Auto Hot Key:
    2.1 Downloading Desktop app for AHK file:
        Auto Hot Key nazort fayllari ishlashi uchun
        quyidagi havola orqali Desktop ilovani PCga
        o'rnatilishi kerak: https://www.autohotkey.com/
    
    2.2 Creating AHK file:
        Yangi Soliq.ahk faylini yaratasiz (muhimi
        fayl kengaytmasi .ahk bo'lishi kerak)
    
    2.3 Juda muhim qismi diqqatli bo'ling:
        scrcpy ochgan ilovani full screen 
        modega qo'yiladi va test uchun kalkulator
        ilovasi ochiladi hamda autohotkeyDash
        hozirgina o'rnatilgan ilovani ochib 
        window spy bo'limi tanlanadi.

    2.4 Koordinatalash qismi:
        Telefonda kalkulator ilovasini ochiladi.
        Keyin esa 1 dan 9 gacha va 0 ( hamda
        tasdiqlash tugmasi ham, buni
        koordinatasi CONFIRM_X v CONFIRM_Y
        biln belgilangan kod qismida ) har bir raqamga 
        sichqonchani birma-bir olib boriladi 
        hamda window spy dagi Mouse Position -> Screen
        koordinatalari birma bir yozib olinadi.

    2.5 Olingan koordinatalar ahk faylda NX va NY 
        parametrlarini o'rniga qo'yiladi va fayl 
        save qilinadi.
        AHK faylini ishga tushurish uchun saqlangan 
        ahk faylning ustiga 2 marta bosiladi.
        Endi fayl ishga tushurilish uchun tayyor.
        va WIN + R => test boshlash uchun
        WIN + S => testni to'xtatish uchun

    2.7 Endi O'z ilovangizga moslab olishingiz va 
        DELAY_MS =120 hamda CLICK_DUR = 30
        kichraytirilgan holatda tez natija olishingiz
        mumkin.

    2.8 Qanchalik tez bo'lmasin, uni kod terishini kuzatib,
        taxminan nechaga kelganini taxmin qilib ham 
        START_NUM qiymatini u taxminiy sondan kichik son 
        bilan tenglab qayta test qilsa bo'ladi.

3. O'zingizga kerakli app uchun:
        (2.2) Creating AHK file dan toki
        (2.8) bo'limgacha qayta bajaring.
